Benvenuti al

Ristorante e Pizzeria Salve D'or

Small culinary monument, settled in one of the most beautiful city in New Zealand, Tauranga. Here you can enjoy Mediterranean and European specialties like "Anelli di Calamari"; "Gamberi Saporiti"; "Bistecca Mignon"; "Lasagne tradizionale al forno" and many other recipes expertly prepared, respecting the authenticity and freshness of the ingredients used.

The Restaurant furnished with antique ornaments evoking pleasant memories of another time, makes the atmosphere even more enchanting, a perfect setting for candlelit dinners and "a la carte" menu.

The Restaurant offers excellent selection of Italian wines and personalized menu for adults and children. All combined with the typical Mediterranean hospitality, warm and attentive, makes Salva D'or a welcoming and hospitable place, ideal for your winter or summer dine in and takeaway evenings.
